Privacy Policy

Our commitment to your privacy is paramount. This Privacy Policy explains how Paramount Computer Diagnostics collects, uses, and protects information.

Information Collection and Use
Paramount Computer Diagnostics collects data from your device to assess job compatibility and update your Bullhorn profile. The collected information includes system hardware details, network information, audio and video input devices, all integrated into your Bullhorn profile through your registered email address with Paramount.

Information Sharing and Disclosure
The collected data is solely used to evaluate candidate eligibility for specific job positions and is not shared with third parties outside the organization, except as necessary to fulfill job-related requests.

Access to Your Information and Control
You have the right to access, correct, or delete your information. Visit our contact page to manage your data.

Security Measures
While we employ security measures to protect your information, avoid transmitting sensitive data via our contact form to mitigate potential risks.

Links to Other Sites
Paramount Computer Diagnostics contains one link to an external site; It is the app's help/support page and is maintained by Paramount.

For any queries regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us through our contact page.
