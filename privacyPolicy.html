Privacy Policy

Our commitment to your privacy is paramount. This Privacy Policy explains how Paramount Computer Diagnostic collects, uses, and protects information.

Information Collection and Use
Paramount Computer Diagnostics collects data from your device to assess job compatibility and update your Bullhorn profile. Following is the specific information collected:

  • System Hardware Details:

    It collects information about which version of Windows you are using, and information regarding your system's hardware, including Machine Type, Active Monitors, Free & Total RAM (memory), CPU Type, Processor, Cores, available Hard Drives, and Antivirus currently being used.

  • Network Information:

    The app also measures your download and upload speeds, the type of Network Connection you have, and your Network Provider.

  • Audio and Video Input Devices:

    It determines the availability of audio (microphone) and video (camera) input devices on your system along with the camera resolution.

  • Bullhorn Integration:

    The collected data can then be integrated into your Bullhorn profile, enhancing its accuracy and completeness. This is done through your email address that is registered with Paramount.

Information Sharing and Disclosure
The collected data is solely used to evaluate candidate eligibility for specific job positions and is not shared with third parties outside the organization, except as necessary to fulfill job-related requests.

Access to Your Information and Control
You have the right to access, correct, or delete your information. Visit our contact page to manage your data.

Security Measures
While we employ security measures to protect your information, avoid transmitting sensitive data via our contact form to mitigate potential risks.

Links to Other Sites
Paramount Computer Diagnostics contains one link to an external site; It is the app's help/support page and is maintained by Paramount.

For any queries regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us through our contact page or the following email, support@hireparamount.com.
