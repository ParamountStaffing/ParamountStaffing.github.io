About the App

Our app assists job candidates in determining their eligibility for positions by evaluating their system specifications. It then updates their Bullhorn profile with the obtained data.

Data Collected by the App

The application gathers various details from your device to assess job compatibility and update your Bullhorn profile if applicable. Here's the specific information collected:

  • System Hardware Details:

    It collects information about which version of Windows you are using, information regarding your system's hardware, including Machine Type, Active Monitors, Free & Total RAM (memory), CPU Type, Processor, Cores, available Hard Drives, and the Antivirus currently being used.

  • Network Information:

    The app also measures your download and upload speeds, the type of Network Connection you have, and your Network Provider.

  • Audio and Video Input Devices:

    It determines the availability of audio (microphone) and video (camera) input devices on your system along with the camera resolution.

  • Bullhorn Integration:

    The collected data is automatically integrated into your Bullhorn profile, enhancing its accuracy and completeness. This is done through your email address that is registered with Paramount.

How It Works

Here is a step by step process of using the app properly. If you havent already downloaded the app, you can do so by clicking on the following link.

  • Step 1: Launching the App

    When you first launch the app, you will see a small pop up window asking for your email address. This is the same email adress that you have registred with Paramount & Bullhorn. After you put in the email adress, you can press the Submit button.

  • Step 2: Accessing System Analysis

    It may take some time for the loading screen to go away but when it does go away, a new window will pop up which will present your system information.

  • Step 3: Submitting Data to Bullhorn

    If you feel like something went wrong or you were connected to the wrong network, you can just press the 'Rescan' button to update the information. If you are satisfied with the data that you see on your screen, you can press the 'Submit' button to submit the information to be updated on our Bullhorn database.
