How It Works

Here is a step by step process of using the app properly.

  • Step 1: Launching the App

    When you first launch the app, you will see a small pop up window asking for your email address. This is the same email adress that you have registred with Paramount & Bullhorn. After you put in the email adress, you can press the Submit button.

  • Step 2: Accessing System Analysis

    It may take some time for the loading screen to go away but when it does go away, a new window will pop up which will present your system information.

  • Step 3: Submitting Data to Bullhorn

    If you feel like something went wrong or you were connected to the wrong network, you can just press the 'Rescan' button to update the information. If you are satisfied with the data that you see on your screen, you can press the 'Submit' button to submit the information to be updated on our Bullhorn database.
